About Me

Who we are

Aryavarta is a Semiconductor Design Services Company​

Established in the year 2017 in the field of Analog Mixed
Signal Design Services and Solutions through the development of high-performance designs for Semiconductor Integrated Circuits (ICs), based out of Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.

The design solutions were provided for various industry verticals like Automotive, Avionics, Consumer Electronics, Industrial, Medical, Wireless Telecom,  Sensors ……

Aryavarta Circuits is a company which is focused on Analog / Mixed Signal IC and RF IC Design Services with a leadership team with 180+ years of Experience in the semiconductor field.

Our Organization

Vision

Partnering with Integrity, Consultant, and Employer Pioneering in the IC design industry in response to the ever-increasing need for qualified personnel. We would like to be on point company with all required solutions for a semiconductor Industries

Mission

Aryavarta Circuits is a developer, integrator and manufacturer of semiconductor devices, Focused, conscious, and ongoing talent

Goals

Our core competency is to provide services which has edge in VLSI and embedded system design services by offering complete ASIC & SoC solutions,
